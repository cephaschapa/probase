SHIKOLA - SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Shikola is a school management system that can be used by; school administrators, teachers, accountants, parents, students, and human resource personnel within K12, Colleges, and Universities.

The system can be used both online and offline. When online, Shikola can be accessed anywhere, and information added will be saved automatically. When offline, it can be accessed within the school premises.
